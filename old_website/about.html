Learn about me.

Presently, I am studying at Brown University and expect to graduate in May 2020 with an Sc.B. in Computer Science. I like to challenge myself by taking on a rigorous load of coursework each semester in addition to the various activities and extracurriculars that I participate in. While I gain the most enjoyment out of taking CS courses (lol), I have also taken several courses in other not so far-removed disciplines such as economics and applied math. In the spirit of Brown's Open Curriculum, I have also dabbled in some english, philosophy, cognitive neuroscience, music, and visual arts.

In my free time, I like to fold origami, practice violin, play tennis/go to the gym with friends, or play Go. I will also play/develop video games and conduct research in Brown's robotics lab. For more information regarding my coursework and activities please see my resume page.
